## Section A — Answer any six Questions (6 × 2 = 12)

1. Answer the following:
   - a. How many odd days are there in 123 days?
   - b. How many odd days are there in 366 days?

2. If 1st January 2020 was a Wednesday, what will be the day of the week on 1st January 2021?

3. A small cafe recorded the number of orders over 3 days:
   - Monday: 120 orders
   - Tuesday: 150 orders
   - Wednesday: 130 orders

   Out of total orders, 40% were online orders. What is the total number of online orders over the 3 days?

4. In a queue of 50 people, A is 16th from the left and B is 19th from the right. How many people are there between A and B?

5. Number of students enrolled in a course over years:
   - 2018: 200
   - 2019: 240
   - 2020: 300
   - 2021: 360

   What is the percentage increase in students from 2018 to 2020?

6. Find the missing term in the series: 6, 18, 36, 60, ?, 126

7. Ravi starts from his house and walks 10 m towards North. He then turns right and walks 5 m, and finally turns right again and walks 10 m. In which direction is Ravi now from his starting point?

8. In a marathon event, Arjun finished 12th from the start, and there were 35 runners in total. What was Arjun's position from the end?

9. If "STAR" is written as "RSZQ" in a certain code, then how will "MOON" be written in that code?

10. Priya starts from her home and walks 12 m towards East. She then turns left and walks 5 m. After that, she turns left again and walks 12 m. How far is Priya from her starting point?
