## Section A — Answer any six Questions (6 × 2 = 12)

1. Answer the following:
   - a. How many odd days are there in a non-leap year?
   - b. How many odd days are there in a leap year?

2. If today is Friday, what day will it be after 199 days?

3. A fruit shop sold apples over 3 days:
   - Monday: 30 apples
   - Tuesday: 45 apples
   - Wednesday: 25 apples

   What is the average number of apples sold per day?

4. In a row of students, P is 14th from the left and Q is 11th from the right. If there are 25 students in total, what is the position of Q from the left?

5. A mobile shop recorded its monthly sales of smartphones over four months:
   - January: 200 units
   - February: 180 units
   - March: 150 units
   - April: 250 units

   What is the percentage decrease in sales from January to March?

6. Find the missing term in the series: ANB, CPD, ERF, ?

7. Joseph starts from a point and walks 14 km towards the west. He then turns right and walks 9 km, turns right again and walks 14 km, and finally turns right and walks 5 km. How far is Joseph from his starting point, and in which direction is he located?

8. Anitha is 8th from the top, and Deepak is 6th from the bottom. If 5 people are ranked between Anitha and Deepak, what is the total number of people in the group?

9. In a certain code, ROMAN is written as "# \ @ % *" and SEA is written as "& ! %". How will "ROSE" be written in that code?

10. A boy starts from home, walks 10 km south, turns right and walks 7 km, then turns right and walks 10 km, and finally turns left and walks 3 km. In which direction is he now from his home?
