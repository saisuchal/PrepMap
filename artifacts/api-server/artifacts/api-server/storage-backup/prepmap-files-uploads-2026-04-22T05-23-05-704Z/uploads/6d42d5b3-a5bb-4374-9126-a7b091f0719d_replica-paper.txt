Part A (3 Marks Each)

1.
A can complete a work in 8 days and B in 12 days. They work together for 3 days, then A leaves. In how many days will B finish the remaining work?

2.
A pipe fills a tank in 10 hours and another empties it in 15 hours. If both are opened together, how long will it take to fill the tank?

3.
12 workers can complete a work in 15 days. After 5 days, 4 workers leave. How many more days will the remaining workers take to complete the work?

4.
Define permutation and combination. Give one real-life example for each.

5. A box contains 4 red, 3 green, and 5 blue balls. Two balls are drawn at random without replacement. Find the probability that both are red.

Part B (5 Marks Each)
6. Two pipes A and B can fill a tank in 20 minutes and 30 minutes respectively. A third pipe can empty it in 60 minutes.
(a) Find the net rate of filling.
(b) Find the time to fill the tank.


7. 20 workers can complete a work in 18 days working 7 hours/day. After 6 days, 5 workers leave and remaining work 9 hours/day.
(a) Work done in first 6 days
(b) Remaining time to complete work

8. From 10 boys and 8 girls, a team of 6 is to be selected:
(a) How many ways to select exactly 3 boys?
(b) How many ways to select at least 2 girls?

9. Arrange letters of the word BALLOON:
(a) Total distinct arrangements
(b) Arrangements where vowels are together

10. A can complete a work in 15 days and B in 20 days. They work together for 4 days, then B leaves.
(a) Remaining work
(b) Total time to finish

11. Two dice are rolled:
(a) Probability that sum is 10
(b) Probability that at least one die shows 6
(c) Probability that numbers are different
