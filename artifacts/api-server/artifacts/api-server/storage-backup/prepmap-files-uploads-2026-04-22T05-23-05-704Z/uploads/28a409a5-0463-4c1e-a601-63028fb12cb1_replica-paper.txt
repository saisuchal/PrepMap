## PART – A (5 × 2 = 10 Marks) — Answer Any Five Questions

1. If a watch shows 2:30 in a mirror, what is the actual time?

2. Answer the following Questions:
   - How many odd days are there in a leap year?
   - How many odd days are there in a Non Leap year?

   - Year 1: ₹2,50,000
   - Year 2: ₹3,00,000
   - Year 3: ₹3,75,000
   - Year 4: ₹4,50,000

   What is the percentage increase in revenue from Year 1 to Year 4?

| C | 175 |

7. Find the next four leap years after 2099, and justify your answer.
